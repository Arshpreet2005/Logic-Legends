About us 24 hours a day, seven days a week, 365 days a year, the National Domestic Violence Hotline provides essential tools and support to help survivors of domestic violence so they can live their lives free of abuse. We offer specialized counseling for women affected by domestic violence, combining trauma-informed care, legal advocacy, and holistic well-being strategies.
